INTERNSHIP

I am pleased to share that I successfully completed a 14-day internship training with the Highways Department of Tamil Nadu (National Highways), Salem, from 29th July 2024 to 11th August 2024.

During this internship, I had the opportunity to:

  • Visit various Civil Engineering construction sites
  • Gain hands-on exposure to road construction, bridge and culvert development
  • Understand real-time construction management practices
  • Observe the planning and execution of highway projects

    • This experience offered me valuable insights into Civil Engineering and Highways Management, bridging the gap between academic learning and real-world applications. I would like to thank the Highways Department, Salem, for this enriching learning experience.
